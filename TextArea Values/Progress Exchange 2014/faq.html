REGISTRATION

How do I register?
You may register for Progress Exchange 2014 by clicking on the Register button or visiting www.progress.com/exchange
I started my registration but didn't finish. How can I complete my registration?
You can revisit the registration page and re-enter your First Name, Last Name and Email address and hit Submit, and the registration system will prompt you through to completion.
I received a discount code. How do I apply it to my registration?
If you have not yet registered for Progress Exchange, begin your registration and insert your discount code on the “Payment page. The system will recalculate your registration fee.

If you have already registered, please forward your discount code to our registration team via email at registration@progress.com. Please allow up to 48 hours for your request to be processed.
What is the payment policy?
Progress Exchange 2014 Registration accepts several methods of payment including checks, purchase orders and all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and American Express). Payment must accompany your registration, or your registration will not be processed. If you are paying by check, please mail your check in US $’s to:
Progress Exchange 2014
Registration Progress Software
14 Oak Park Drive
Bedford, MA 01730 USA
What is the cancellation policy?
All cancellations must be received in writing via email. Send your cancellation notice to registration@progress.com. For a full refund, cancellations must be received by 12:00 noon (EDT) on Friday, September 12, 2014. No refunds will be given after 12:00 noon (EDT) on Friday, September 12, 2014.
Are attendee substitutions permitted?
If you’re unable to attend Progress Exchange, you may transfer your registration to another person. Please email registration@progress.com with the new attendee's name and contact information by Friday, October 3, 2014. Substitutions will not be granted after this date.
If I cancel my registration, can I transfer my hotel reservation to another attendee?
Yes. Please work directly with the hotel to transfer your reservation and to provide the new attendee’s credit card information to secure their reservation.
Can I bring a guest?
Guest passes are available for $75 for the MIX@Exchange Welcome Reception on Monday, October 6, and $150 for the PARTY@Exchange on Wednesday, October 8. Guests will not be permitted to attend any of the Progress Exchange conference sessions, workshops or meals. Children under 21 years of age are not eligible to attend the conference.

TRAVEL AND HOTEL

Where is the Hilton Orlando located?
Hilton Orlando is located at 6001 Destination Parkway, Orlando, Florida, USA, in close proximity to Orlando’s main attractions, shopping and dining. Map… Please note: Hilton owns more than one hotel in the city of Orlando. Please be sure you reserve your room at the Hilton Orlando.
Where can I find information about Orlando?
For information on things to do during your stay in Orlando, please visit http://www.visitorlando.com/plan-your-trip/visitor-center/
What is the weather like in Orlando in October?
Most would agree that October is a great time of the year to visit Orlando. The average temperature during the day is in the mid-80s °F (approx. 30°C), and evening temps range from the mid-to-high 60s°F (18 - 21°C). Please check www.weather.com before you arrive.
Which airport is closest to Orlando?
Orlando International Airport (MCO) is the closest major airport, an easy 12-mile drive to/from the conference hotel.
How do I arrange ground transportation?
Mears Transportation offers a shared ride shuttle that departs from the airport approximately every 30 minutes. For shuttle information and reservations, visit www.hello-use.com/tickets/progress14/
Are there special hotel rates?
Yes, there are discounted sleeping room rates for Progress Exchange attendees at the Orlando Hilton for $185 plus tax per night. To receive these special rates, once you have completed the conference registration process, go to the “Venue” page and you can reserve a room using the Passkey link provided. Or, you can contact the hotel by phone and indicate you’ll be attending Progress Exchange 2014.
For Hilton Orlando Reservations: call +407.313.4300 or toll-free at 888-488-3509
The discounted hotel rate for Progress Exchange is available until September 15, 2014. After this date, the rate will be determined by the hotels.
I’ve already registered for Progress Exchange. How can I add a hotel reservation to my registration or make changes to my existing hotel reservation?
To modify or cancel your existing reservation, please contact the hotel directly.
What is the hotel room cancellation policy?
If you booked a reservation at the discounted conference rate, cancellations must be made directly with the hotel no fewer than 48 hours prior to your arrival. Failure to do so will result in a penalty by the hotel equal to the cost of one night's room and tax.
If I cancel my registration, can I transfer my hotel reservation to another attendee?
Hotel reservations made through Progress Exchange registration are not transferable, but you can contact the hotel directly to modify your reservation and/or provide the name and credit card information for the substituting attendee.
What if I don’t check into the hotel on my scheduled arrival date?
If you don’t arrive on your scheduled arrival date, the hotel will cancel your entire reservation and can opt not to reinstate your reservation, even if you arrive the next day. You will also be charged the penalty of the cost of one night's room and tax, regardless of whether or not the hotel opts to reinstate your reservation.
Can I pay the hotel with a different credit card than the one I provided when I reserved the room?
Yes. However, if the card used as a guarantee for your hotel reservation is not in your name, that cardholder must be present at check-in, or a faxed credit card authorization form must be set up with the hotel prior to your arrival.

How do I register for sessions or workshops?
All of the breakout sessions offered at Exchange are open to all attendees. No additional registration/reservation is required. For the technical workshops, your conference fee entitles you to sign-up for one workshop on Thursday, October 9. You may select your desired workshop during the registration process. Workshops are available on a first-come, first-served registration basis. If you wish to add a workshop at a later date or switch to a different workshop, you may revisit the registration page and “edit” your workshop selection based on availability.
I have been waitlisted for a workshop. What happens next?
If you wish to attend a workshop and were notified the course is full, our registration team will contact you if space becomes available.
Can I cancel my workshop?
If you will not be attending a workshop, email your cancellation to registration@progress.com.
